Our Story

A journey rooted in passion for exceptional food and warm hospitality

In the heart of San Juan's prestigious Condado neighborhood, PRIME GRILL 1302 stands as a testament to the belief that great dining brings people together. Our story begins with a simple yet profound vision: to create a steakhouse where premium quality meets family warmth.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to excellence in every detail. From hand-selecting the finest cuts of prime beef to sourcing fresh ingredients from Puerto Rico's best local farms and fisheries, we ensure that every dish that leaves our kitchen meets the highest standards.

Our master chefs bring decades of culinary expertise, blending traditional steakhouse techniques with innovative island-inspired flavors. The result is a menu that honors classic favorites while celebrating the vibrant tastes of Puerto Rico.
